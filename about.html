Who we are

Welcome to our family-owned bio farm! We are a unique farm in that we have a deep respect for our land and the environment, and as such, we do not allow vehicles on our fields. Our farm has been in operation since 1850, and we are proud to say that we are now in our 5th generation of family ownership and leadership.

Our farm is located in a picturesque countryside, surrounded by rolling hills and lush green fields. We specialize in producing organic and sustainable crops, and our commitment to responsible farming practices is evident in everything we do.

At our farm, we believe in the importance of preserving our land for future generations. That's why we have adopted a number of eco-friendly practices, including crop rotation, composting, and natural pest control methods. Our commitment to sustainability doesn't stop there - we also use renewable energy sources and strive to minimize waste wherever possible.

We take pride in our farm-to-table approach, and we love sharing our produce with our community. Whether you're looking for fresh, organic vegetables, or pasture-raised chicken eggs, we have something for everyone. And if you're interested in learning more about our farming practices, we offer events throughout the year.

We invite you to come visit us and see for yourself what makes our farm so special. From our family to yours, we look forward to welcoming you to our farm!

Bio and organic certifications

At our family-owned bio farm, we are proud to have both bio certifications and organic certifications, which sets us apart from many other farms. These certifications are a testament to our commitment to sustainable and responsible farming practices.

Our bio certification means that we meet strict European Union standards for organic agriculture, including the use of natural fertilizers, crop rotation, and natural pest control methods. We are committed to using sustainable farming methods that preserve the health of our soil, protect our local ecosystem, and promote biodiversity.

Our organic certification is another mark of our commitment to producing high-quality, healthy food. We do not use synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs) on our crops or animals. Instead, we rely on natural methods to maintain healthy soil and control pests.

We take pride in our certifications, which demonstrate our dedication to sustainable farming practices and the production of healthy, organic food. Our customers can trust that when they purchase products from our farm, they are getting the highest quality farming product.

Our certifications are not just a label - they are a way of life at our farm. We believe that by working in harmony with nature, we can produce food that is both delicious and nutritious, while also promoting the health of our environment and our local community. Come visit us and see for yourself why our bio and organic certifications are so special!
